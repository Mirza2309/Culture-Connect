SARHUL: THE SURREAL FESTIVAL

SOYAM PRABHA MALLICK
#sarhul #festivalofflowers #jharkhand

I have recently been to Jharkhand and I got the priviledge to witness the amazing festival of Sarhul. Sarhul is celebrated by many tribes in Jharkhand, but most notably the Munda, Ho, and Oraon tribes. While nature veneration is an important aspect of the festival, there are also many cultural programmes that are enjoyed along with it. Among these many cultural events is "Baa Porob", the festival of flowers and the worship of the Sal Tree as well as a special Sarhul dance.The festivities around Sarhul are made even more special with the many festive delicacies that are cooked and enjoyed, such as the rice dish called "handia", a baked or dried fish dish called "fish sukha".
